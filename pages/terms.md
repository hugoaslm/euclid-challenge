# Terms and Conditions

By participating in this challenge, you agree to the following rules.

## Scientific use
The dataset originates from the **Euclid Q1 data release** and associated value-added catalogs.  
Participants must use the data **only for research and educational purposes** within the scope of this challenge.

The data must not be redistributed outside the competition.

## Fair use of the platform
Participants agree to:

- Submit original models and code
- Avoid any attempt to exploit the evaluation system
- Not reverse-engineer or infer the private test labels

Any attempt to manipulate the leaderboard may result in disqualification.
